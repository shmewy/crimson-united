Our Policies

Committed to a Brighter Future






Defense

Our defense policy is focused on maintaining a strong and secure nation by increasing our nuclear weaponry while also protecting our nation by creating more anti-nuclear weapons. We aim to keep our military ready for any challenge while working toward a safer world for future generations.

Immigration

We believe in reforming our immigration system to decrease illegal immigration and encourage more legal pathways for people to come here. By strengthening our borders and simplifying legal processes, we can create a fair and balanced system that welcomes those who follow the rules.

Foreign Affairs

Supporting our allies is central to our foreign policy. We will stand by our international partners, promoting peace and stability across the globe while defending our shared interests and values.

Economy

To strengthen our economy, we will tackle inflation and follow the principles of Reaganomics, focusing on lowering taxes, reducing government spending, and creating more opportunities for growth. This approach will help families and businesses thrive in a more prosperous economy.
